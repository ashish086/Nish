Poetry Competition

Poetry Competition:

On the occasion of the 2nd Anniversary of Nishritha Foundation some events were organised. Poetry competition was one of them. The events were conducted between 11 June,2020 - 14 June,2020. The theme of the competition was PERFECT IMPERFECTION. It was organised on 12 June 2020 with free registration. The event had some guidelines and criteria which were as follows.

GUIDELINES.

  • ● Entries were accepted only through Google form.
  • ● Language can be either Hindi or English. Both categories will be judged separately.
  • ● Poems can be 8-12 lines long at maximum.
  • ● All participants were required to follow the Nishritha Foundation on Instagram.
  • ● The submitted content should be an original piece of work.


CRITERIA.
  • ● Originality and Creativity.
  • ● In accordance with the given theme.
  • ● Certificate of participation was provided to every participant.
  • ● Top 5 poems from both the categories were featured on the Instagram account of Nishritha Foundation.


WINNERS.
English Category- Elina Singh and Soumya Kewat.
Hindi Category - Vaishnavi and Khushi.
