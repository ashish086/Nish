Yash-o-Utsav


We organized a 7 days utsav named, Yash-o-Utsav from October 02, 2020 to October 07, 2020
The entertaining events organized under Yash-o-Utsav were fundraising events.
Money raised through these events shall be utilized for the upliftment of hearing-impaired children.
Many people participated along with their friends and families. They helped us reach many people who need us and are still out there .

Due to Yash-o-Utsav, we have raised Rs. 13,000 that shall be used in accomplishing our Vision.
