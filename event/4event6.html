Vitual Run Away!:

The Fourth event of Yash-O-Utsav was Virtual Run AwayAway. It was the Virtual Marathon in which the participants were supposed to run a total distance of 5km with minimum duration. This event took place on October 6, 2020 and this event conducted through Just Move App platform.
S.Pooja and Bhavna awarded as winners of Virtual Run Away event and they rewarded the cash prize of Rs.500 each.
This event provided a great opportunity to many people to explore their talents. Long runs plays a big role in helping you hone your mental toughness.
