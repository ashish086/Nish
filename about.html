About Us

Nishritha Foundation is a registered NGO determined to work for the upliftment of hearing-impaired children by educating and providing them proper training to make them capable of joining the mainstream of society. With the same motivation in mind, Nishritha Foundation was established by our CEO Selvamani Ramanathan in 2018. Nishritha means essence or the core nature which defines that at root we all are humans and we have that willpower and capability of doing wonders, it just needs that one extra push in the case of these children. With this belief in mind we help them with every possible tool which enables them to shape their lives, to grow and most important to be independent.
